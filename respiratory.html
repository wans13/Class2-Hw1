The Respiratory System

The respiratory system is an organ system that is responsible for breathing and gas exchange.

The organs that make up the respiratory system include:

  • Lungs
  • Nose
  • Mouth
  • Throat(Pharynx)
  • Windpipe(Larynx)
  • Bronchi
  • Bronchioles

The respiratory system uses the lungs to draw in air from your surroundings and to perform gas exchange.
