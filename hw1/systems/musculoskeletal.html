The Musculoskeletal System

The musculoskeletal system and organ system that is responsible for your body structure and moving your body around.

The organs that make up the musculoskeletal system include:

  • All of your bones
  • All of your muscles, tendons, and ligaments
    • (There are too many to list)

The bones give structure to your body, the muscles allow for movement, the tendons connect the muscles to the bones, and the ligaments connect your bones.
